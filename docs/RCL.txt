RECALLS

The RECALL file contains all NHTSA safety-related defect and compliance 
campaigns since 1967.

File characteristics:

-  All the records are TAB delimited
-  All dates are in YYYYMMDD format

-  Maximum Record length: 17,108

FIELDS:
=======

Field#   Name                Type/Size   Description                      
------   ---------           ---------   --------------------------------------
1        RECORD_ID           NUMBER(9)   Running Sequence Number, Which Uniquely Identifies The Record

3        MAKETXT             CHAR(25)    Vehicle/Equipment Make
4        MODELTXT            CHAR(256)   Vehicle/Equipment Model

6        MFGCAMPNO           CHAR(20)    Manufacturer Campaign Number

10       ENDMAN              CHAR(8)     End Date of Manufacturing
11       RCLTYPECD           CHAR(4)     Vehicle, Equipment, Child Restraint, Or Tire Part 573 Defect/Noncompliance Report

13       ODATE               CHAR(8)     Date Manufacturer Notified Owners
14       INFLUENCED_BY       CHAR(4)     Recall Initiated By (MFR/OVSC/ODI)
15       MFGTXT              CHAR(40)    Manufacturers of Recalled Vehicles/Equipment/Child Restraint/Tires
16       RCDATE              CHAR(8)     Part 573 Defect/Noncompliance Report Received Date
17       DATEA               CHAR(8)     Record Creation Date
18       RPNO                CHAR(3)     Regulation Part Number

20       DESC_DEFECT         CHAR(6000)  Defect Summary

28		 DO_NOT_DRIVE		 CHAR(3)	 Consumer Advisory: Do Not Drive
